Our Partners

At Sodbridge Academy, our partnerships represent a cornerstone of our commitment to our student success. We collaborate with leading organizations and industry professionals who share our vision of equipping learners with real-world skills and opportunities. These partners not only support our mission but also play an active role in providing internship placements, hosting mock interviews, and preparing our graduates for the job market. By showcasing our growing network of partners, we aim to inspire confidence in our students and demonstrate the tangible career pathways available through our programs.

Jobberman

Jobberman is Nigeria’s leading job placement platform, connecting top talent with career opportunities. Through our partnership, students gain access to internship listings, career readiness tools, and job market insights.

Interview Master

InterviewMaster.ai is the job preparation platform that has helped thousands of data job seekers to prepare for technical interviews. It features realistic interview practice questions, adaptive learning pathways, and instan deep feedback to help professionals master their SQL interviews.

Homefort Energy

Homefort is a clean tech company revolutionizing how businesses access and manage cooking gas. Through our proprietary hardware and software, we’ve built an intelligent gas delivery system that ensures customers never run out of gas - and never overpay.

Loger

Loger makes finding your dream home simple by matching you with trusted agents who meet your budget and location needs. Let us take the hassle out of house hunting. Their mission is to revolutionize the property search process by making it easy and efficient for both property seekers and agents

Yisada Technology

Yisada Technology is a forward-thinking AI and software development company based in Germany. The company specializes in delivering innovative digital solutions tailored to help businesses navigate and excel in the tech-driven landscape with key focus on Software Development, AI & Chatbots development, Data Analytics, and IT consulting.
